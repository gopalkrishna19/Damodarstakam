Damodarastakam

The Divine Chase

A sacred game inspired by the pastimes of Nandagaon. Mother Yashoda chases her beloved Krishna through the village.

How to Play

  • Yashoda (Player 1) moves first
  • Take turns moving to adjacent nodes
  • Yashoda wins by catching Krishna
  • Krishna wins by evading capture
