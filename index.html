Damodarastakam

Overview

A divine chase in Nandagaon. Player 1 is Mother Yashoda, Player 2 is Krishna.

How to Play

  • Yashoda moves first.
  • Players take turns moving to adjacent nodes.
  • Yashoda wins if she catches Krishna (lands on the same node).
  • Krishna wins if he evades capture for the duration of the game.
